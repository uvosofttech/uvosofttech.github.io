How it works

Simple process. Clear approvals. Professional deliverables.

1) Submit manuscript

Send your manuscript via WhatsApp (or email). Tell us genre + goal + deadline. If you have cover ideas, share them too.

2) We build the publishing package

Editing/proofreading (if needed), formatting (Kindle/ePub/Print), plus cover design options and a clean structure.

3) You approve → we deliver

You review drafts. We apply final changes. You receive final files + assets ready for publishing and promotion.
